JOURNEY OF MY EDUCATION 🎓


You can click on this map to see where I studied

  1. Primary school: SK JALAN PEEL, Maluri

    2. My education journey began at SK Jalan Peel, a place that holds so many special memories for me. I spent seven years here, growing and learning not just academically but also about the importance of friendships, teamwork, and having fun.

    • Time period: since 2009-2016
    • Memories:
      • Joined Hari Kanak-Kanak (Children's Day) celebrations filled with fun activities and performances.
      • Participated in a mini zoo exhibition, where various animals were showcased to educate students.
      • Served as a school prefect from Year 2 to Year 6, learning leadership and responsibility.
    • Achievement:
      • Received the Excellence Award (Anugerah Cemerlang) every year throughout my time at SK Jalan Peel, recognizing my academic achievements and dedication.

  2. High school: SMK CONVENT JALAN PEEL, Cheras

    3. High school was a transformative time for me, both academically and personally. At SMK Convent Jalan Peel, I began to explore my passions and interests while building lifelong friendships.

    • Time period: since 2017-2021
    • Memories:
      • The last day before SPM preparations remains a cherished memory, filled with heartfelt moments with classmates.
      • Served as the class monitor from Form 3 to Form 5, developing leadership and organizational skills.
      • Form 5 memories were significantly impacted by the Covid-19 lockdown, which resulted in limited time at school and a shift to online learning.
    • Favourite Subject: Mathematics and Chemistry
    • Achievement:
      • Participated in the STEM Carnival during Form 3 by managing a booth and receiving a certificate for the effort.
      • The class was awarded "Most Cheerful Class" and had the highest attendance in every grade.

  3. Diploma: UiTM Kampus Rembau, Negeri Sembilan

    4. After high school, I took the next big step in my education journey: university life at UiTM Kampus Rembau. This phase of my education was an exciting time filled with opportunities, challenges, and growth.

    • Time period: since 2022-2025
    • Memories:
      • Experiencing the scenic surroundings of Rembau, with a mix of quiet study time and fun outings with friends.
      • Adjusting to university life and bonding with dorm mates during late-night study sessions and campus activities.
    • Favourite Subject: Subjects like Programming Fundamentals and Database Management
    • Achievement:
      • Awarded Dean's List recognition for four consecutive semesters for outstanding academic performance.
