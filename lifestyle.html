Cooking Recipess 🍳

  1. SPAGHETTI CARBONARA 🍝

    2. Ingredients:

    • 200g spaghetti
    • 2 eggs
    • 4 slices cheese
    • 4 slices of minced beef meat (chopped)
    • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
    • Salt and pepper to taste

    Steps:

    1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water. Drain and reserve 1 cup of pasta water.
    2. In a bowl, whisk eggs and 4 slices cheese together.
    3. Fry the minced beef meat in a pan until crispy. Add garlic briefly.
    4. Turn off the heat, add the spaghetti, and toss with the meat.
    5. Quickly pour in the egg mixture, stirring fast to avoid scrambling. Add reserved pasta water to adjust the sauce's creaminess.
    6. Season with salt and pepper, then serve immediately.

  2. BAKING COOKIES 🍪

    3. Ingredients:

    • 125g butter (softened)
    • 100g sugar
    • 1 egg
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • 200g all-purpose flour
    • 1/2 tsp baking powder
    • 1 tbs chocolate chips

    Steps:

    1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Line a baking tray with parchment paper.
    2. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
    3. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract.
    4. Gradually add flour and baking powder, mixing until a dough forms.
    5. Fold in chocolate chips.
    6. Scoop dough onto the baking tray and flatten slightly.
    7. Bake for 10–12 minutes or until golden brown. Cool before serving.

  3. BUTTERMILK CHICKEN 🍗

    4. Ingredients:

    • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
    • 1/2 cup milk
    • 2 tbsp butter
    • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
    • 1/2 cup evaporated milk
    • 1/4 cup water
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • chilli powder

    Steps:

    1. Marinate chicken in buttermilk for at least 30 minutes.
    2. Fry the chicken until golden and cooked through. Set aside.
    3. In a pan, melt butter and garlic until fragrant.
    4. Add evaporated milk, water, and chili sauce. Stir and season with salt and pepper.
    5. Simmer for 2–3 minutes, then toss the chicken in the sauce until well coated.
    6. Serve hot with rice or bread.
